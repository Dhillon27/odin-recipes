Pizza

Description

Be it any occasion, we all love to eat pizza at home. But, what goes behind making this delicious dish? Well, learn all about making a delicious pizza at home with this easy recipe that will take you through detailed steps.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 100ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • chilli flakes
  • sugar
  • 100gm processed cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Spread the sauce on the base
  4. Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes
